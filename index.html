FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before working with Alpha26.

How long does a typical project take?
With our AI-first approach, timelines are dramatically shorter. A simple web app ships in 2–4 weeks; a full mobile platform in 6–12 weeks. After discovery, we give you a precise timeline with milestones before a single line of code is written.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
Both! We've built MVPs for seed-stage startups and enterprise platforms for 500+ employee companies. Our flexible models — fixed price, T&M, dedicated team — adapt to any stage and budget.
What makes your AI-first approach different?
We don't just use ChatGPT. We have a fully orchestrated AI layer: AI code generation with Copilot and Cursor, AI-generated test suites with Testim and Applitools, AI-driven CI/CD with Harness, and AI monitoring with Datadog. The result is 10× speed with higher quality.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Every project includes 30-day warranty. We also offer AMC packages covering bug fixes, security patches, AI-driven monitoring, and feature enhancements. We're partners, not just builders.
How do you handle IP ownership and security?
All code, designs, and assets are 100% yours on final payment. We sign NDAs before discovery, follow OWASP standards, run AI security scans on every codebase, and can comply with GDPR, HIPAA, or any framework you need.
What is your pricing model?
Fixed-price for well-defined projects; time-and-material for evolving scopes. After a free discovery call, we provide a detailed itemised proposal — no hidden charges, ever. Our AI efficiency often means we cost 30–40% less than comparable studios.
